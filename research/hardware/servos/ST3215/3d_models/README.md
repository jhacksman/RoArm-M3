## Dimensions for Manual Modeling

If you need to create your own 3D model, here are the key dimensions:
- Length: 40 mm
- Width: 20 mm
- Height: 40 mm
- Shaft Diameter: 6 mm
- Mounting Hole Pattern: Standard servo mount
